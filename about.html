About The Business

25+ Years of Experience

American Diesel Mechanix was founded on the belief that quality repair shouldn't require a tow truck. With over 25 years of hands-on experience in the mechanic field, we have seen it all.


We specialize in big rig machines, dump trucks, and heavy machinery, but we apply that same level of care and expertise to regular passenger vehicles. Whether you are a fleet manager or a commuter stranded on the roadside, we have the tools and the know-how to get you back on the road.

  • Expertise: Specialized in Heavy Duty Diesel.
  • Local: Serving NE Florida for decades.
