Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work on passenger cars?

Yes. While we specialize in heavy-duty diesel and semi-trucks, we are fully equipped to repair regular cars, pickup trucks, and SUVs.

What is your service area?

We serve St. Johns County, Duval County, Putnam County, and the immediate surrounding areas.

Do I need to tow my vehicle to you?

No! We are a mobile mechanic service. We travel to your location, whether it's roadside, a job site, or your home driveway.

How do I schedule service?

The fastest way is to call us directly at 386-227-0152.
